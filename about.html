ABOUT US

OLLYSAN RECRUIT ENTERPRISES LIMITED

Ollysan Recruit Enterprises Limited was established by individuals with Sanusi Ayobami as the Head. it was formed as a result of the disappointment encountered in themanner at which the nation handles the youth development and sports. This is an organization that is formed for the development of youths and sports by providing for them an environment that enables maturity development, creativity and the fear of God. The organization through one of its agencies Ollysan Recruit Sports Academy (ORSA) is affiliated to Oyo State Sports Council.

VISION

Education is the foremost in developing and transforming lives for the advancement of a better society and a peaceful world. Youths are the most vulnerable to the vices of our society.

Our vision is to create the environment suitable for growth and development of youths and sports through the formation of moral conscience and right mind.

The environment is said to form the character of a person.

MISSION

  • To train, educate and develop the youths, using the best methology and latest technology, hardworking, honest, humble, physically and morally sound individuals

  • To enjoy all beenfit of life that comes with playing the game (i.e. Competition, Success, Health,Performance)

  • To campaign on the prevention of drug abuse in sports, cultism in schools, child abuse sex education and HIV/AIDS.

  • To motivate youth on how to be responsible, honest and good citizens

  • To be eligible to play and study through scholarships in or outside Nigeria

  • To teach principles, strategies and gamemanship of the sports
